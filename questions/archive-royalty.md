## H4
Q: Who was the first female pharaoh of ancient Egypt?
A) Ankhesanamun
B) Cleopatra
C) Hatshepsut
D) Nefertiti
Answer: C
Fact: Because no son was born to her, Thutmose II passed the throne to a son of a lesser haram queen. She started as a queen regent (acting in stead of the king) but managed to gain the crown in her seventh year of power. Thutmose III eventually regained power after her death, and successfully erased all traces of her rule until recently in 1822.
