# Category: 2000s Pop

## E1
Q: Which artist released the 2003 album 'Dangerously in Love'?
A) Rihanna
B) Beyoncé
C) Alicia Keys
D) Mariah Carey
Answer: B
Fact: 'Dangerously in Love' won five Grammy Awards, making it one of the biggest album launches of the decade.

## E2
Q: Which pop star sang '...Baby One More Time'?
A) Christina Aguilera
B) Pink
C) Britney Spears
D) Jessica Simpson
Answer: C
Fact: '...Baby One More Time' was rejected by TLC before Britney Spears recorded it and turned it into a global phenomenon.

## E3
Q: Which boy band sang 'Bye Bye Bye' in 2000?
A) Backstreet Boys
B) 98 Degrees
C) Westlife
D) NSYNC
Answer: D
Fact: 'Bye Bye Bye' sold over one million copies in its first week, setting a record for a single at the time.

## E4
Q: Which singer released 'Beautiful' from the 2002 album 'Stripped'?
A) Pink
B) Nelly Furtado
C) Demi Lovato
D) Christina Aguilera
Answer: D
Fact: 'Beautiful' won the Grammy for Best Female Pop Vocal Performance and became an anthem for self-acceptance worldwide.

## E5
Q: Nelly's 2002 smash 'Hot in Herre' appeared on which album?
A) Country Grammar
B) Nellyville
C) Da Derrty Versions
D) Sweat
Answer: B
Fact: 'Hot in Herre' sampled Chuck Brown's 'Bustin' Loose' and topped charts in over a dozen countries simultaneously.

## E6
Q: Which Canadian artist sang 'Complicated' in 2002?
A) Carly Rae Jepsen
B) Nelly Furtado
C) Avril Lavigne
D) Alanis Morissette
Answer: C
Fact: Avril Lavigne was just 17 when 'Complicated' was released, and it became one of the best-charting debut singles of the decade.

## E7
Q: Which artist released 'Crazy in Love' featuring Jay-Z in 2003?
A) Destiny's Child
B) Ciara
C) Beyoncé
D) Aaliyah
Answer: C
Fact: 'Crazy in Love' interpolates the Chi-Lites' 1970 song 'Are You My Woman' and is widely regarded as one of the greatest pop songs of the decade.

## E8
Q: Which group sang the 2001 anthem 'Survivor'?
A) TLC
B) En Vogue
C) Destiny's Child
D) SWV
Answer: C
Fact: 'Survivor' was widely interpreted as a response to the public fallout with former members who had sued the group.

## E9
Q: Eminem's 2002 hit 'Without Me' appeared on which album?
A) The Marshall Mathers LP
B) Recovery
C) The Slim Shady LP
D) The Eminem Show
Answer: D
Fact: 'The Eminem Show' debuted at number one in over 25 countries and became one of the best-selling albums of 2002.

## E10
Q: Which Irish band had a hit with 'Beautiful Day' in 2000?
A) Coldplay
B) U2
C) The Cranberries
D) Snow Patrol
Answer: B
Fact: 'Beautiful Day' won three Grammy Awards including Record of the Year and helped cement U2's continued dominance in the 2000s.

## M1
Q: Jay-Z and Linkin Park released which mash-up album in 2004?
A) Collision Course
B) Numb/Encore
C) Reanimation
D) Blueprint 2
Answer: A
Fact: 'Collision Course' debuted at number one and won a Grammy for Best Rap/Sung Collaboration for its lead single 'Numb/Encore'.

## M2
Q: Which Outkast double album contained both 'Hey Ya!' and 'The Way You Move'?
A) ATLiens
B) Aquemini
C) Speakerboxxx/The Love Below
D) Stankonia
Answer: C
Fact: 'Hey Ya!' was a solo André 3000 track from 'The Love Below' side, while 'The Way You Move' belonged to Big Boi's 'Speakerboxxx' disc.

## M3
Q: Which pop-rock band released 'Mr. Brightside' in 2003?
A) Fall Out Boy
B) The Killers
C) My Chemical Romance
D) Panic! at the Disco
Answer: B
Fact: 'Mr. Brightside' spent a record-breaking 260+ weeks on the UK charts and is considered one of the most-streamed songs of the 2000s era.

## M4
Q: Amy Winehouse's debut album 'Frank' was released in which year?
A) 2001
B) 2004
C) 2003
D) 2006
Answer: C
Fact: 'Frank' was named after jazz legend Frank Sinatra and showcased Amy Winehouse's jazz and soul roots before her breakthrough 'Back to Black'.

## M5
Q: Which artist's R&B debut album was 'Can't Take Me Home' in 2000?
A) Alicia Keys
B) Ashanti
C) Pink
D) Keyshia Cole
Answer: C
Fact: Pink's debut was primarily R&B-influenced before she switched to her signature rock-pop sound on her second album 'Missundaztood'.

## M6
Q: Who featured on Mariah Carey's 'Say Somethin'' from her 2005 comeback album?
A) Kanye West
B) Snoop Dogg
C) Pharrell
D) Jadakiss
Answer: B
Fact: Mariah Carey's 'The Emancipation of Mimi' became the best-selling album of 2005 in the United States after years of personal difficulties.

## M7
Q: Which singer released 'Fallin'' from her self-titled debut album in 2001?
A) Jill Scott
B) Erykah Badu
C) Alicia Keys
D) India.Arie
Answer: C
Fact: Alicia Keys was just 20 years old when 'Songs in A Minor' debuted at number one, selling 236,000 copies in its first week.

## M8
Q: Which indie band released the 2004 single 'Float On'?
A) Death Cab for Cutie
B) Modest Mouse
C) The Shins
D) Wilco
Answer: B
Fact: 'Float On' was Modest Mouse's first mainstream hit, breaking them into the Billboard Hot 100 after years as an indie cult favourite.

## M9
Q: Beyoncé's 2006 hit 'Irreplaceable' was written by which artist?
A) Timbaland
B) Rodney Jerkins
C) Ne-Yo
D) The-Dream
Answer: C
Fact: Ne-Yo originally wrote 'Irreplaceable' for himself but Beyoncé recorded it, turning it into one of the longest-running number-one singles of 2006.

## M10
Q: Nelly Furtado's 2006 Timbaland-produced comeback album was titled what?
A) Whoa Nelly!
B) Folklore
C) Mi Plan
D) Loose
Answer: D
Fact: 'Loose' produced three consecutive top-five UK singles: 'Maneater', 'Say It Right', and 'All Good Things (Come to an End)'.

## H1
Q: Justin Timberlake's 'FutureSex/LoveSounds' featured which producer most prominently?
A) Pharrell
B) Timbaland
C) Kanye West
D) Rick Rubin
Answer: B
Fact: Timbaland co-wrote and produced the bulk of 'FutureSex/LoveSounds', cementing his status as the defining producer sound of mid-2000s pop.

## H2
Q: Maroon 5 formed in which US city before releasing 'Songs About Jane'?
A) New York
B) Chicago
C) Los Angeles
D) Atlanta
Answer: C
Fact: Maroon 5 began as a high-school band called Kara's Flowers in Los Angeles before rebranding and achieving mainstream success in 2002.

## H3
Q: Lily Allen's 2006 debut single, which hit number one in the UK, was called what?
A) Littlest Things
B) LDN
C) Smile
D) Knock 'Em Out
Answer: C
Fact: Lily Allen originally posted 'Smile' on MySpace before being signed — it reached number one in the UK in July 2006.

## H4
Q: Which rapper featured on Akon's 2006 hit 'Smack That'?
A) T-Pain
B) Eminem
C) Ludacris
D) 50 Cent
Answer: B
Fact: 'Smack That' reached number two on the Billboard Hot 100 and helped formalise the partnership between Akon's Konvict label and Eminem's Shady Records.

## H5
Q: Which artist's album 'Graduation' debuted the same week as 50 Cent's 'Curtis' in 2007?
A) Lil Wayne
B) Jay-Z
C) Kanye West
D) T.I.
Answer: C
Fact: 'Graduation' outsold 'Curtis' by nearly 300,000 copies in their shared debut week, prompting 50 Cent to (temporarily) pledge retirement from solo albums.

## H6
Q: Fergie's 2007 hit 'Glamorous' featured which rapper?
A) Ludacris
B) T-Pain
C) Pitbull
D) Flo Rida
Answer: A
Fact: 'Glamorous' was co-written by will.i.am and Fergie, and Ludacris's verse playfully references his own humble origins despite his then-massive fame.

## H7
Q: Rihanna's debut single 'Pon de Replay' was released in which year?
A) 2006
B) 2003
C) 2004
D) 2005
Answer: D
Fact: Rihanna was discovered by Def Jam CEO Jay-Z at just 16 — she auditioned with 'Pon de Replay' and was signed on the spot.

## H8
Q: Which UK act released the 2004 concept album 'A Grand Don't Come for Free'?
A) Arctic Monkeys
B) The Streets
C) Dizzee Rascal
D) The Libertines
Answer: B
Fact: 'A Grand Don't Come for Free' follows a single narrative about a lost £1,000 note and is considered one of the most ambitious British pop albums of the 2000s.

## H9
Q: Which UK artist's 2008 single 'American Boy' features Kanye West?
A) Estelle
B) Shingai
C) Beverley Knight
D) Sade
Answer: A
Fact: 'American Boy' won the Grammy for Best Rap/Sung Collaboration in 2009, giving British singer Estelle her biggest international breakthrough.

## H10
Q: T-Pain's 2007 hit 'Buy U a Drank' featured which rapper?
A) Lil Wayne
B) Yung Joc
C) Young Jeezy
D) Ludacris
Answer: B
Fact: T-Pain's Auto-Tune style became so iconic in the late 2000s that 'T-Pain effect' became a standard vocal preset in music production software.
