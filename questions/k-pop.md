# Category: K-Pop

## E1
Q: Which K-pop boy group is known for the hit 'Dynamite'?
A) EXO
B) BTS
C) BIGBANG
D) GOT7
Answer: B
Fact: Dynamite was BTS's first song recorded entirely in English and debuted at number one on the Billboard Hot 100.

## E2
Q: BLACKPINK has how many members?
A) 3
B) 5
C) 4
D) 6
Answer: C
Fact: BLACKPINK's four members are Jisoo, Jennie, Rose, and Lisa, all trained by YG Entertainment.

## E3
Q: BTS is managed by which Korean agency?
A) SM Entertainment
B) YG Entertainment
C) JYP Entertainment
D) HYBE
Answer: D
Fact: HYBE was formerly known as Big Hit Entertainment before rebranding in 2021 as it expanded into a multi-label company.

## E4
Q: Which girl group released the iconic song 'Gee'?
A) 2NE1
B) f(x)
C) Girls' Generation
D) Wonder Girls
Answer: C
Fact: Gee spent nine consecutive weeks at number one on the Gaon Digital Chart, a record at the time of its release in 2009.

## E5
Q: What is the official fandom name of BTS?
A) Blink
B) Army
C) Once
D) Reveluv
Answer: B
Fact: ARMY stands for Adorable Representative MC for Youth, and the fandom is consistently ranked among the most powerful in the world.

## E6
Q: Which artist is known for the hit 'Gangnam Style'?
A) Super Junior
B) SHINee
C) PSY
D) 2PM
Answer: C
Fact: Gangnam Style became the first YouTube video to reach one billion views in 2012, breaking the platform's view counter at the time.

## E7
Q: Which BLACKPINK member is from New Zealand?
A) Jisoo
B) Lisa
C) Jennie
D) Rose
Answer: D
Fact: Rose, born Roseanne Park, grew up in Auckland and Melbourne before moving to South Korea to train with YG Entertainment.

## E8
Q: Girls' Generation and EXO are both from which agency?
A) JYP Entertainment
B) HYBE
C) Starship
D) SM Entertainment
Answer: D
Fact: SM Entertainment, founded in 1995, is one of Korea's oldest agencies and helped define the idol training system used across the industry.

## E9
Q: Which group performed the hit 'TT'?
A) IZ*ONE
B) TWICE
C) ITZY
D) Mamamoo
Answer: B
Fact: TT's music video reached 100 million YouTube views faster than any previous K-pop girl group video at the time of its 2016 release.

## E10
Q: What is the fandom name for BLACKPINK fans?
A) Once
B) Blink
C) Shawol
D) Army
Answer: B
Fact: The name BLINK is a blend of BLACK and PINK, the two colours in the group's name, and was officially announced in 2017.

## M1
Q: In what year did EXO debut?
A) 2010
B) 2014
C) 2012
D) 2011
Answer: C
Fact: EXO debuted in January 2012 originally as two subunits: EXO-K performing in Korean and EXO-M performing in Mandarin Chinese.

## M2
Q: Which BTS member is nicknamed the 'Golden Maknae'?
A) V
B) Jimin
C) J-Hope
D) Jungkook
Answer: D
Fact: Jungkook earned the nickname 'Golden Maknae' because he excels at singing, dancing, rapping, and art despite being the youngest member.

## M3
Q: TWICE is signed to which entertainment company?
A) SM Entertainment
B) Cube Entertainment
C) HYBE
D) JYP Entertainment
Answer: D
Fact: TWICE was formed through JYP's 2015 survival show Sixteen, hosted by JYP founder Park Jin-young himself.

## M4
Q: Which group released 'Monster' on their 2016 album 'Ex'act'?
A) Super Junior
B) EXO
C) SHINee
D) BIGBANG
Answer: B
Fact: EXO's Ex'act album sold over one million copies in under a week, cementing them as one of K-pop's most consistent million-sellers.

## M5
Q: SEVENTEEN is a boy group under which agency?
A) Pledis
B) FNC
C) Starship
D) Woollim
Answer: A
Fact: Although Pledis was acquired by HYBE in 2020, SEVENTEEN continues to operate under the Pledis sub-label within the HYBE umbrella.

## M6
Q: What is the official fandom name of EXO?
A) EXO-L
B) Shawol
C) Inspirit
D) MyDay
Answer: A
Fact: EXO-L means EXO-Love, and the letter L sits between K and M in the alphabet, symbolising the bridge between EXO-K and EXO-M.

## M7
Q: Which member of TWICE is from Taiwan?
A) Jihyo
B) Tzuyu
C) Mina
D) Sana
Answer: B
Fact: Tzuyu, whose full name is Chou Tzu-yu, is the youngest member of TWICE and was born in Tainan, Taiwan.

## M8
Q: In what year did Girls' Generation debut?
A) 2009
B) 2005
C) 2007
D) 2011
Answer: C
Fact: Girls' Generation debuted on August 2, 2007 with the single 'Into the New World', which later became a beloved anthem at Korean protest movements.

## M9
Q: Which group had a massive hit with 'Growl' in 2013?
A) SHINee
B) BIGBANG
C) EXO
D) Super Junior
Answer: C
Fact: Growl became a defining song of K-pop's fourth-year idol cycle and is still performed at EXO concerts as one of their signature anthems.

## M10
Q: Which fourth-generation group debuted in 2020 from HYBE's BELIFT LAB?
A) TOMORROW X TOGETHER
B) ENHYPEN
C) ZEROBASEONE
D) BOYNEXTDOOR
Answer: B
Fact: ENHYPEN was formed through the 2020 survival show I-Land and debuted in November that year, rising rapidly on global charts.
