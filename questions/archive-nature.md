A) Aloe Vera
B) Cacti
C) Snake plant
D) Jade Plant
Answer: C
Fact: Snake plants are also known as "mother-in'law's tongue" are hardy and thrive in both shade and direct sunlight. They actually make the perfect houseplant as they can produce oxygen even at night through a process called "Crassulacean Acid Metabolism" instead of respiring. It's estimated that you need about 8 plants per person to survive in a sealed room and it doubles as a natural air freshener by absorbing toxins like formaldehyde and paint fumes to improve air quality
