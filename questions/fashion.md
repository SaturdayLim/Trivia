# Category: Fashion

## E1
Q: Which country is luxury brand Gucci from?
A) France
B) Italy
C) Spain
D) Switzerland
Answer: B
Fact: Guccio Gucci founded the brand in Florence in 1921, originally selling leather luggage.

## E2
Q: Which brand is famous for its iconic red-soled shoes?
A) Manolo Blahnik
B) Jimmy Choo
C) Christian Louboutin
D) Stuart Weitzman
Answer: C
Fact: Louboutin painted his assistant's nail polish on the sole of a prototype in 1992, and the red sole was born.

## E3
Q: Which country is Chanel based in?
A) Italy
B) UK
C) France
D) Germany
Answer: C
Fact: Gabrielle 'Coco' Chanel opened her first shop in Paris in 1910, starting with hats.

## E4
Q: Who founded the fashion house Louis Vuitton?
A) Coco Chanel
B) Christian Dior
C) Louis Vuitton
D) Pierre Cardin
Answer: C
Fact: Louis Vuitton moved to Paris at age 16 and became a master trunk-maker before founding his brand in 1854.

## E5
Q: Burberry is best known for its signature what?
A) Polka dots
B) Tartan plaid
C) Houndstooth
D) Paisley print
Answer: B
Fact: Burberry's beige, black, red, and white check pattern became one of the most recognisable prints in fashion.

## E6
Q: Which country is the luxury brand Hermès from?
A) Italy
B) France
C) Belgium
D) Portugal
Answer: B
Fact: Thierry Hermès founded the brand in Paris in 1837 as a harness and saddle workshop.

## E7
Q: What two letters form the iconic Chanel logo?
A) CC
B) GG
C) LV
D) YSL
Answer: A
Fact: The interlocking double-C monogram was introduced by Coco Chanel herself in the 1920s.

## E8
Q: The Birkin bag is made by which luxury house?
A) Prada
B) Gucci
C) Hermès
D) Dior
Answer: C
Fact: The Birkin was designed in 1984 after actress Jane Birkin complained to Hermès CEO Jean-Louis Dumas about her overflowing straw bag.

## E9
Q: Nike's famous logo is called what?
A) Flash
B) Tick
C) Swoosh
D) Wave
Answer: C
Fact: The Swoosh was designed by graphic design student Carolyn Davidson in 1971 for just $35.

## E10
Q: What was Coco Chanel's real first name?
A) Catherine
B) Gabrielle
C) Marguerite
D) Jacqueline
Answer: B
Fact: 'Coco' was a nickname she picked up while performing as a cabaret singer in her early years.

## M1
Q: Versace was founded by which designer?
A) Donatella Versace
B) Gianni Versace
C) Roberto Cavalli
D) Giorgio Armani
Answer: B
Fact: Gianni Versace launched his label in Milan in 1978; his sister Donatella took over after his death in 1997.

## M2
Q: Which country is the luxury brand Prada originally from?
A) France
B) Spain
C) Italy
D) UK
Answer: C
Fact: Mario Prada founded the brand in Milan in 1913 as a leather goods and luggage shop.

## M3
Q: Who was Yves Saint Laurent's longtime business partner and co-founder?
A) Karl Lagerfeld
B) Pierre Bergé
C) Marc Jacobs
D) Hubert Givenchy
Answer: B
Fact: Bergé and Saint Laurent founded the YSL fashion house in 1961 and remained partners for nearly 50 years.

## M4
Q: Which brand uses interlocking 'LV' monogram as its signature print?
A) Loewe
B) Lanvin
C) Louis Vuitton
D) Longchamp
Answer: C
Fact: Georges Vuitton introduced the LV monogram canvas in 1896 to combat counterfeiting of his father's trunks.

## M5
Q: The 'Kelly bag' is named after which famous personality?
A) Grace Kelly
B) Kelly Clarkson
C) R. Kelly
D) Ned Kelly
Answer: A
Fact: The Hermès bag was renamed the Kelly in 1956 after Princess Grace of Monaco used it to shield her pregnancy from photographers.

## M6
Q: Which city is Dolce & Gabbana based in?
A) Rome
B) Venice
C) Naples
D) Milan
Answer: D
Fact: Domenico Dolce and Stefano Gabbana met in Milan and launched their label there in 1985.

## M7
Q: For roughly how many decades did Karl Lagerfeld lead Chanel?
A) Two
B) Three
C) Four
D) Five
Answer: B
Fact: Lagerfeld joined Chanel in 1983 and led the house until his death in 2019 — a 36-year reign.

## M8
Q: Ralph Lauren was born with what original surname?
A) Lipschitz
B) Goldstein
C) Rosenberg
D) Bernstein
Answer: A
Fact: Ralph Lifshitz changed his surname to Lauren in his teens; he launched Polo Ralph Lauren in 1967.

## M9
Q: Lacoste's polo shirt features which animal on its chest?
A) Penguin
B) Alligator
C) Crocodile
D) Lizard
Answer: C
Fact: Tennis star René Lacoste earned the nickname 'The Crocodile' on court, and the emblem was placed on his shirts from 1933.

## M10
Q: Which Swedish city is H&M headquartered in?
A) Gothenburg
B) Malmö
C) Uppsala
D) Stockholm
Answer: D
Fact: Erling Persson founded the brand in Västerås in 1947 as 'Hennes' (meaning 'Hers'), before it became H&M in 1968.

## H1
Q: Who founded the luxury house Givenchy?
A) Hubert de Givenchy
B) Philippe de Givenchy
C) Louis de Givenchy
D) Jean de Givenchy
Answer: A
Fact: Hubert de Givenchy founded his house in Paris in 1952 and became Audrey Hepburn's favourite designer.

## H2
Q: Bottega Veneta was founded in which Italian city?
A) Florence
B) Vicenza
C) Bologna
D) Padua
Answer: B
Fact: Founded in Vicenza in 1966, Bottega Veneta is renowned for its intrecciato woven leather technique.

## H3
Q: Which designer served as creative director of Balenciaga before Demna Gvasalia took over in 2015?
A) Hedi Slimane
B) Alexander Wang
C) Nicolas Ghesquière
D) Raf Simons
Answer: C
Fact: Nicolas Ghesquière led Balenciaga from 1997 to 2012, earning global praise for his futuristic, architectural designs before moving to Louis Vuitton.

## H4
Q: Fendi's iconic double-F logo was designed by which legendary designer?
A) Karl Lagerfeld
B) Valentino Garavani
C) Giorgio Armani
D) Gianni Versace
Answer: A
Fact: Lagerfeld designed the interlocking double-F 'Fun Fur' logo for Fendi in 1965 and remained their creative director for 54 years.

## H5
Q: Salvatore Ferragamo began his career making shoes in which US city?
A) New York
B) Los Angeles
C) Chicago
D) Santa Barbara
Answer: D
Fact: Ferragamo moved to Santa Barbara in 1914 to work with his brothers, and went on to make shoes for Hollywood stars.

## H6
Q: Hermès's iconic 'Carrée' product is which type of item?
A) Belt
B) Scarf
C) Glove
D) Wallet
Answer: B
Fact: The Hermès silk square scarf, or 'carré', launched in 1937 and is still hand screen-printed with up to 40 colours.

## H7
Q: The luxury house Loewe was founded in which country?
A) Portugal
B) Germany
C) Spain
D) Austria
Answer: C
Fact: Loewe was founded in Madrid in 1846 by a collective of Spanish leather artisans before a German entrepreneur took it over.

## H8
Q: Who was the first female creative director of Givenchy?
A) Phoebe Philo
B) Clare Waight Keller
C) Sarah Burton
D) Stella McCartney
Answer: B
Fact: Clare Waight Keller was appointed at Givenchy in 2017 and also designed Meghan Markle's royal wedding gown in 2018.

## H9
Q: Miuccia Prada holds a doctorate in which academic field?
A) Art History
B) Philosophy
C) Political Science
D) Semiotics
Answer: C
Fact: Before taking over Prada, Miuccia earned a PhD in political science from the University of Milan and trained as a mime artist.

## H10
Q: The luxury goods conglomerate LVMH has its headquarters in which city?
A) London
B) Geneva
C) Paris
D) Milan
Answer: C
Fact: LVMH — Moët Hennessy Louis Vuitton — was formed in 1987 and is now the world's largest luxury goods group by revenue.
