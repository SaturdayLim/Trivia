## H2
Q: Which song contains the lyrics "Eleka nahmen nahmen, ah tum ah tum, eleka nahmen"?
A) For Good
B) March of the Witch Hunters
C) No Good Deed
D) Ozdust Duet
Answer: C
Fact: This was the incantation used to keep Fiyero from dying, inadvertantly turning him into a scarecrow that could feel no pain.

## H3
Q: Which song contains the lyrics "I've got one more night left here in town"
A) A Sentimental Man
B) Dancing Through Life
C) No One Mourns the Wicked
D) Something Bad
Answer: C
Fact: This line was sung as Glinda retold the conception of Elphaba, when her mother's lover had one last night in town.

## H4
Q: Which of the following do not belong to the same song?
A) Clandestinedly
B) Dialysis
C) Perfunctorily
D) Unprepossessing
Answer: C
Fact: The rest of these words came from the song "Popular", sung by Elphaba and Glinda.

## H5

A) Dancing Through Life
B) Defying Gravity
