B) French

## M3
Q: What is the country of Genovia known for?
A) Cheese
B) Olives
C) Pears
D) Wine
Answer: C
Fact: Genovia’s pear obsession is a running gag across the films — from state dinners to official exports — and was chosen deliberately because it sounds quaint, royal, and just odd enough to stick in your memory without being distracting.
