# Category: Sitcoms

## E1
Q: In "The Office" (US), which of the following is not the name of a main character working there?
A) Jim Halpert
B) Oscar Martinez
C) Kevin Malone
D) David Brent
Answer: D
Fact: David Brent is actually the main character in the UK version, played by Ricky Gervais.

## E2
Q: In "Modern Family", how many people are in the title family?
A) Five
B) Nine
C) Twelve
D) Fifteen
Answer: C
Fact: Gloria married Jay whose kids (Claire and Mitchell) married Phil and Cameron respectively. Claire and Phil have three kids - Haley, Alex and Luke, making them a family of five. Mitchell and Cameron adopted a girl - Lily, making them a family of three. Jay and Gloria had a kid (Joe) besides Manny, making them a family of four and bringing the total to 12.

## E3
Q: In "Friends", which of the friends was a chef?
A) Chandler
B) Joey
C) Monica
D) Phoebe
Answer: C
Fact: From a young age, Monica had a passion for first eating, and then cooking. Her mother taught her how to cook, and she pursued it as a career, eventually becoming head chef at various restaurants throughout the series.

## E5
Q: What does the Bluth family-owned company, the Bluth Company, primarily do?
A) Frozen Bananas
B) Real Estate
C) Banking

## E6
Q: In the show, what is the Bluth family's favored mode of transportation?
A) Stair car
B) Segways
C) Limousine
D) Company Jet

Fact: Three different truck cabs were used over the filming of the series, all Ford F350s. A 1978 or '79 model, a mid-1980s model, and a 1967-72 model updated with a late '70s grille.
