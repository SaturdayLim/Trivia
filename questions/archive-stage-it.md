## E3
Q: Who was cast as the voice of "Elsa" in the movie release of Frozen?
A) Idina Menzel
B) Kristen Bell
C) Kristin Chenoweth
D) Kristen Stewart
Answer: A

## H3
Q: Finish the lyrics to this Hamilton song, Lafayette: I'm taking this _____________
A) course of events
B) city by the state
C) horse by the reins
D) torch to the gates
Answer: C
Fact: Daveed Diggs delivers Lafayette’s fastest verse at around 6.3 syllables per second, which puts it in the same range as elite competitive rap. Miranda intentionally packed in consonant-heavy phrasing so that even native English speakers struggle to parse individual words.
