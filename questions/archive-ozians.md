## H3
Q: One of the original film actors had to be recast 9 days into filming for health reasons. What role was this for?
A) Cowardly Lion
B) Dorothy
C) Scarecrow
D) Tin Man
Answer: D
Fact: Buddy Ebsen was originally cast as scarecrow but swapped to the Tin Man role. The aluminum powder in the silvery makeup poisoned his lungs, leaving him hospitalized and in an Iron lung.
