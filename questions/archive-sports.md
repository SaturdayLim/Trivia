## E1
Q: Cleats are used in some sports for a better grip on soft surfaces. Which of the following do not require these spiked boots?
A) Baseball
B) Floorball
C) Soccer
D) Sprinting
Answer: B
Fact: The spikes are used to dig into loose terrain, like grass or synthetic tracks. Games played on smooth courts like a gym or rink need flat rubber soles with rounded edges to grip the floor.

## E3
Q: The final pose of a yoga session is where the yogi is prone on their back, arms aside and legs fully extended. What pose is this called?
A) Extended Pose
B) Field Pose
C) Corpse Pose
D) Warrior 0
Answer: C
Fact: Also called "Shavasana", lying still in this pose allows the body to redistribute the energy stored up in any one part and release tension or stress. Although some see it as a yogic faux pas, falling asleep in this pose of rest is quite ideal for those who see it as a time to relax. If your goal is to consciously observe thoughts, perhaps the seated lotus position would be better!

## H2
Q: Modern boxing is scored on a "10-point Must System", where the winner gets 10 points. If the round is dominated by a huge margin but a knockdown is not scored, how many points does the loser get?
A) Zero
B) Four
C) Six
D) Eight
Answer: D
Fact: Most rounds are scored 10-9, and knockdowns take another point off the loser's score. Negative scores cancel each other out, but when judges spot a clear margin that round, they would award 10-8 points to the fighters. Aggregate scores after 10 or 12 rounds determine the winner.
