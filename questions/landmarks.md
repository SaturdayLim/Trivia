# Category: Landmarks

## E1
Q: Which country is the Eiffel Tower in?
A) Italy
B) France
C) Spain
D) Belgium
Answer: B
Fact: The Eiffel Tower was built as the entrance arch for the 1889 World's Fair in Paris.

## E2
Q: The Great Wall is found in which country?
A) Japan
B) Mongolia
C) China
D) South Korea
Answer: C
Fact: The Great Wall stretches over 21,000 kilometres when all its sections are added together.
