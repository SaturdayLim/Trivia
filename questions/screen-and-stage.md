## E1
Q: In "The Wizard of Oz," what color are the shoes Dorothy wears?
A) Silver
B) Red
C) Gold
D) Blue
Answer: B
Fact: In the original book the slippers were silver; the film made them ruby red.

## E2
Q: Which animated movie features a clownfish named Nemo?
A) Finding Nemo
B) Shark Tale
C) Moana
D) Up
Answer: A
Fact: The film's coral reef setting was inspired by Australia's Great Barrier Reef.

## E3
Q: What do we call a theatre performer's rehearsal without an audience?
A) Premiere
B) Rehearsal
C) Encore
D) Intermission
Answer: B
Fact: Some early rehearsals happen with no scenery at all, called a "table read."

## M4
Q: In music and theatre, what does the audience ask for when they shout "encore"?

B) For the performer to perform again

Fact: The word "encore" comes from the French for "again."
