# Category: Lyrics

## E1
Q: Complete the lyric: 'She works hard for the ___.' (Donna Summer)
A) Money
B) Weekend
C) Glory
D) Future
Answer: A
Fact: Donna Summer wrote this song after observing a tired bathroom attendant at a restaurant in 1983.

## E2
Q: Complete: 'We will, we will ___ you.' (Queen)
A) Love
B) Rock
C) Find
D) Bless
Answer: B
Fact: Brian May designed 'We Will Rock You' so stadium audiences could clap and stomp along without needing instruments.

## E3
Q: Complete: 'Hit me baby ___ more time.' (Britney Spears)
A) Just
B) One
C) Two
D) Once
Answer: B
Fact: The song was originally offered to TLC, who turned it down before Britney Spears recorded it in 1998.

## E4
Q: Complete: 'Don't stop ___, hold on to the feelin'.' (Journey)
A) Dreaming
B) Singing
C) Believin'
D) Running
Answer: C
Fact: 'Don't Stop Believin'' became one of the best-selling digital singles of all time, decades after its 1981 release.

## E5
Q: Complete: 'And I will always love ___.' (Whitney Houston, 1992)
A) You
B) Him
C) More
D) Us
Answer: A
Fact: The song was originally written and recorded by Dolly Parton in 1973; Whitney's version became even more iconic.

## E6
Q: Complete: 'Just a small-town girl, living in a ___ world.' (Journey)
A) Lonely
B) Modern
C) Changing
D) Material
Answer: A
Fact: 'Don't Stop Believin'' opens with this line, making 'lonely world' one of rock's most famous opening images.

## E7
Q: Complete: 'Tell me why, ain't nothin' but a ___.' (Backstreet Boys, 1999)
A) Heartache
B) Mistake
C) Memory
D) Heartbreak
Answer: A
Fact: 'I Want It That Way' topped charts in over 25 countries and is often cited as one of the greatest pop songs ever written.

## E8
Q: Complete: 'Sweet home ___.' (Lynyrd Skynyrd)
A) Georgia
B) Chicago
C) Alabama
D) Carolina
Answer: C
Fact: Lynyrd Skynyrd were actually from Florida, not Alabama, making the song an ode to the broader American South.

## E9
Q: Complete: 'Ain't no mountain high enough, ain't no ___ low enough.' (Marvin Gaye & Tammi Terrell)
A) River
B) Valley
C) Road
D) Tide
Answer: B
Fact: The song was written by Nickolas Ashford and Valerie Simpson, who later performed it as a celebrated duo themselves.

## E10
Q: Complete: 'Ice ice ___.' (Vanilla Ice)
A) Cold
B) Cool
C) Baby
D) Cream
Answer: C
Fact: 'Ice Ice Baby' was the first hip-hop single ever to top the Billboard Hot 100 chart, reaching number one in 1990.

## M1
Q: Complete: 'Every breath you take, every move you ___, I'll be watching you.' (The Police)
A) Take
B) Make
C) Fake
D) Break
Answer: B
Fact: Sting wrote 'Every Breath You Take' in a single morning while staying at Ian Fleming's Jamaican estate, GoldenEye.

## M2
Q: Complete: 'Roxanne, you don't have to put on the ___ light.' (The Police)
A) Green
B) Red
C) Neon
D) Blue
Answer: B
Fact: Sting was inspired by the red lights of Parisian establishments he noticed while on tour in 1978.

## M3
Q: Complete: 'Girls just wanna have ___.' (Cyndi Lauper)
A) Love
B) Fun
C) More
D) Time
Answer: B
Fact: Robert Hazard originally wrote the song from a male perspective; Cyndi Lauper rewrote it to be from a female point of view.

## M4
Q: Complete: 'Blame it all on my roots, I showed up in ___.' (Garth Brooks, 'Friends in Low Places')
A) Jeans
B) Boots
C) Suits
D) Late
Answer: B
Fact: 'Friends in Low Places' became one of the best-selling country singles ever and remains a karaoke staple worldwide.

## M5

B) Me
C) See
D) Free

Fact: Aretha Franklin's 'Respect' was originally recorded by Otis Redding; her feminist reinterpretation became far more famous.

## M6

A) Team
B) School
C) Teen
D) Young
Answer: C
Fact: The title came from a friend writing 'Kurt smells like Teen Spirit' on a wall, referring to a popular deodorant brand.

## M7

A) Strong
B) Alive
C) Cool
D) Free
Answer: B
Fact: Medical professionals use 'Stayin' Alive' to teach CPR compression rhythm as it is almost exactly 103 beats per minute.

B) Go-Go
C) Sleep
D) Dream
Answer: B
Fact: George Michael wrote the song after seeing Andrew Ridgeley's note that accidentally repeated 'go-go' instead of just 'go'.

## M9
Q: Complete: 'I don't want no scrub, a scrub is a guy that can't get no ___ from me.' (TLC)

B) Cash

D) Ride
Answer: A
Fact: 'No Scrubs' was written by Kandi Burruss and Kevin Briggs, and won the Grammy for Best R&B Song in 2000.

## M10
Q: Complete: 'I'm on the right track, baby, I was born this ___.' (Lady Gaga)

B) Strong

Fact: 'Born This Way' sold over one million copies in just five days, setting a record on iTunes at the time of release.

Fact: Kerry Livgren wrote 'Carry On Wayward Son' in a few hours, and it became one of the most-played classic rock songs ever.
