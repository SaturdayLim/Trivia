## H6
Q: Guan Yu's famous weapon, the Green Dragon Crescent Blade, is often associated with him. What is the other name for this weapon?
A) Frost Fair Blade
B) Black Tortoise Blade
C) Demon Slaying Blade
D) Purple Cloud Blade
Answer: A
Fact: The Green Dragon Crescent Blade (Chinese: 靑龍偃月刀) is a guandao wielded by the Chinese general Guan Yu in the 14th-century historical novel Romance of the Three Kingdoms. It is also sometimes referred to as the Frost Fair Blade (冷豔鋸), from the idea that during a battle in the snow, the blade continuously had blood on it; the blood froze and made a layer of frost on the blade.
