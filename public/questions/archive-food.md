## M5
Q: Which term refers to the process of browning meat by inducing a reaction between an amino acid and a reducing sugar?

B) Malliard Reaction
C) Dry Aging
D) Caramelization
Answer: B
Fact: Caramelization also refers to the decomposition and condensation of stabilized sugars, like when toasting marshmallows. The Maillard reaction is also a browning but completely different process that uses heat and requires an amino acid. Interstingly, making caramel with milk and sugar is considered a maillard reaction, not caramelization, due to the protein available in milk.

## H1
Q: Rubbing your hands on any of the following will remove the garlic smell from your fingers except which?
A) Coffee Grounds
B) Garlic Skin
C) Stainless Steel
D) Toothpaste & Mouthwash
Answer: B
Fact: The smell is actually from sulfuric compounds (released when cutting into garlic, fish and onions) that turn into sulfuric acid on contact with water on our hands. These compounds also bind with stainless steel, coffee grounds, and toothpaste, making them all effective scent removers. Some catalogues even sell bars of steel as a kind of soap.
