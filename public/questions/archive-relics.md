## M1
Q: In the Wizard of Oz, what colour was Dorothy's shoes?
A) Gold
B) Ruby
C) Silver
D) Yellow
Answer: C
Fact: The iconic "ruby slippers" were only coloured so because the executives of the first movie were excited about the recently released Technicolour technology.

## M2
Q: What spell did Elphaba cast that for which she was labelled as "Wicked"?
A) Explosion spell
B) Flying spell
C) Tin Man spell
D) Scarecrow spell
Answer: B
Fact: She used it to cause the monkey-servants to grow wings, and was labelled as a witch who had done this on purpose

## M3
Q: What did Glinda give Elphaba that became a symbol of their friendship?
A) Black Hat
B) Broomstick
C) Brooch
D) Glasses
Answer: A
Fact: Although it was initially donated out of ugliness, this first gift was proudly worn by Elphaba until she disappeared at the end.

## M4
Q: In the broadway musical Wicked, what colour is Glinda dressed in?
A) Blue
B) Pink
C) White
D) Yellow
Answer: A
Fact: There were legal issues (MGM holding the image of a pink Glinda) with using Pink in the Broadway design, but Universal was able to secure the clearance to honour the original image of Glinda in the 1939 movie.
