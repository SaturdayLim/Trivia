## E1
Q: Which pop star had a hit single "… Baby One more Time" in 1999?
A) Britney Spears
B) Christina Aguilera
C) Jessica Simpson
D) Mandy Moore
Answer: A
Fact: This song was Britney's debut single, and it topped the Billboard Hot 100 for 2 weeks in 1999

## E2
Q: Which musical features the song Memory, famously sung by the character Grizabella?
A) Cats
B) Evita
C) Les Miserables
D) Phantom Of The Opera
Answer: A
Fact: Memory is one of the most recorded songs from a musical, with interpretations by Barbra Streisand, Elaine Paige, and even Barry Manilow. Its haunting melody has made it an iconic show tune worldwide.

## M5

## H2
Q: Which SHINee song was released in 2015 with a smooth, deep-house style?
A) Married to the Music
B) Dream Girl
C) Lucifer
D) View
Answer: D
Fact: View is widely credited with popularising deep house in mainstream K-pop. Its minimalist beat, airy vocals, and groove-focused structure were a sharp contrast to the maximalist EDM trends of the time, and it influenced a wave of deep house tracks from other idol groups after.
