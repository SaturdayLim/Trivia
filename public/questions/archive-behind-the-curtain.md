## H2
Q: Wicked began as book in 1995 before its adaptation on Broadway. What inspired this book?
A) Murder of a Kid
B) Murder of a Black Man
C) Embassy Bombing
D) Department Store Bombing
Answer: A
Fact: Gregory Maguire was inspired by the 1993 murder of James Bulger, where a young child was murdered by another child. This prompted the thought "could kids be born evil or did circumstance push them towards this?"

## H3
Q: The Wonderful Wizard of Oz was written as a standalone book in 1900 by Frank Baum, expanded upon in further books when met with unexpected success. How many books are in this series?
A) 6
B) 12
C) 40
D) 100
Answer: C
Fact: Dubbed "The Famous Forty" by fans, these were continued almost annually and considered canonical Oz texts.
