## H8
Q: Which of the following songs was the subject of a defamation lawsuit that was eventually dismissed?
A) Barbie Girl (Aqua)
B) Cinderella (Britney Spears
C) Hotel California (Eagles)
D) Mr Brightside (The Killers)
Answer: A
Fact: Contrary to popular belief, the song Barbie Girl had no relation to the toy company at all. In 1997, Mattel filed a trademark violation claim for turning Barbie into a sex object by referring to her as a "Blonde Bimbo", and tarnished their reputation and marketing plan. MCA  (Aqua's label) then launched a counterclaim for defamation when they used the words "piracy" and likened them to a bank robber. The judge dismissed both suits and concluded his ruling with "The parties are advised to chill".
