## E3
Q: What is the element that needs to be present for a compound to be considered "Organic"?
A) Au, Gold
B) C, Carbon
C) Mg, Magnesium
D) O, Oxygen
Answer: B
Fact: The 4 valence electrons on the Carbon-12 isotope allow it to make up to 4 bonds, with many elements. Unlike other elements that form flat or linear shapes with 1/2/3 bonds, this ability allows them to make 3d shapes. Also, carbon-carbon bonds are strong enough to be stable while staying rearrangeable under the right conditions, making it ideal building blocks.
