## H7
Q: Who directed the epic film "Ben-Hur" (1959), which won a record 11 Academy Awards?
A) Alfred Hitchcock
B) Stanley Kubrick
C) William Wyler
D) David Lean
Answer: C
Fact: "Ben-Hur" is a 1959 American epic religious film directed by William Wyler, produced by Sam Zimbalist, and starring Charlton Heston as the title character. A remake of the 1925 silent film with a similar title, it was adapted from Lew Wallace's 1880 novel Ben-Hur: A Tale of the Christ.
