# Category: Villains

## E1
Q: Which villain says 'I'll be back' in The Terminator?
A) T-1000
B) Skynet
C) T-800
D) Reese
Answer: C
Fact: Arnold Schwarzenegger initially wanted to say 'I will be back' but director James Cameron insisted on the contraction.

## E2
Q: Who is the villain in The Lion King?
A) Gaston
B) Scar
C) Jafar
D) Ursula
Answer: B
Fact: Scar was originally named 'Claudius' in early drafts, a nod to the Hamlet villain Claudius.
